Best Breeds for Beginners!

For beginners the size, temperament, and price of the breed can vary. All these things must be carefully taken into account when seeking a specific breed for any novice. In the same way that you must find the right car or even job that best suits you, the same can said about the wonder that is the horse.

Size(Not too big, not too small)

These breeds are ranked from smallest to LARGEST. In this case its okay to be Goldie Locks and pick the right size for you.

Price(The Almighty Dollar)

The most modest option will probably be the mustang as shown below. The typical price can range between $125 to $5000. After the lovely Mustang the Quarter horse breed will be the next best bet coming in at around $3000 on average. And finally last but not least the Paint horse known for its jaw dropping patterns is usually around $5000.
